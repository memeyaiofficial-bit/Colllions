About

The person behind MemeyAI

Collins Mwangi is a Kenyan technology entrepreneur, author, speaker, and the Founder & CEO of MemeyAI Group, an AI-powered technology company building digital products that help businesses and communities across Africa grow through artificial intelligence, software, media, and digital innovation.

Driven by a vision to make technology accessible and practical, Collins founded MemeyAI to solve real-world problems through scalable digital solutions. Under his leadership, the company has developed products spanning AI, education technology, legal technology, digital marketing, business networks, fintech, healthcare, and creator tools — serving businesses, schools, entrepreneurs, and communities across Africa.

Collins is passionate about empowering African entrepreneurs, SMEs, and creators with affordable technology that enables growth, efficiency, and innovation. His work focuses on AI adoption, startup ecosystems, digital transformation, creator monetization, and building products designed specifically for African markets.

Beyond business, Collins is an advocate for youth empowerment, innovation, and community building. He regularly speaks on entrepreneurship, AI, product development, leadership, and the future of technology in Africa — with a mission to inspire a new generation of founders to build globally competitive companies from Africa.

A student of Information Technology at KCA University, Collins combines technical expertise with entrepreneurial thinking. Outside of work, he enjoys hiking, golf, biking, writing, comedy, and mentoring young innovators, and openly credits his Christian faith as the foundation of his leadership and purpose.
